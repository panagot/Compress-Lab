# PRODUCT.md — Compress Lab

## Register
Hybrid: **brand** (landing / grant pitch) + **product** (recipes + lab workbench).

## Product Purpose
Open builder lab that makes Solana **ZK Compression** (Light) and **Token-2022 Confidential Transfers** usable for Anchor/TypeScript developers in 30–60 minutes — via opinionated recipes, a local developer workbench, and workshop content. Does **not** invent new cryptography or replace Light Protocol.

## Users
- Solana Anchor/TypeScript builders (hackathons, startups)
- Grant reviewers (Foundation + regional programs)
- Workshop facilitators (including Superteam Balkan as one channel)

## Branding rule
Chrome and product name stay **global** (Compress Lab · Solana DX). Regional angles live in workshop copy and grant tracks — not in the logo subtitle.

## Tone
Precise, instrumental. Honest about scope (wrappers + recipes, not Circom).

## Anti-references
Purple SaaS, neon crypto casino, “ZKKit invents ZK” marketing, Balkan-only branding when applying ecosystem-wide.
