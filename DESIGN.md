# DESIGN.md — Compress Lab

## Scene
A builder at a stone desk overlooking the Adriatic at blue hour — salt air, cool ink, one copper lamp. Instrument, not casino.

## Color strategy
**Committed:** Aegean ink carries the surface; **compressed teal** is the action accent (~12%); copper wire for warnings/cost deltas only.

| Token | OKLCH / hex intent |
|-------|-------------------|
| paper | cool salt `#E9EEEA` |
| ink | deep Aegean `#0C242E` |
| mist | `#C5D0CB` |
| teal | `#0F8F7F` |
| teal-deep | `#0A5C52` |
| copper | `#B56A3A` |
| code-bg | `#10252E` |

## Typography
- Display: **Syne** (geometric, distinctive)
- Body longform: **Source Serif 4**
- UI / labels: **DM Sans** (not Inter)
- Code: **IBM Plex Mono**

## Layout
Editorial split + ledger index. Cards only for interactive lab steps. Macro whitespace. Hairline rules, not boxed grids.

## Motion
Ease-out expo; fade/translate only. Staggered recipe rows. Lab step progression with status pulse.
